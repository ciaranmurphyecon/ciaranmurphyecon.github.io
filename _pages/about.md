Hi, I'm Ciaran, a PhD candidate in Economics at University College Dublin (UCD), specialising in applied microeconomics with expertise in causal inference methods. My research sits at the intersection of labour and education economics.

Previously, I completed an MSc in Economics at Maynooth University (First Class Honours) and a Bachelor of Business Studies at Dublin City University (First Class Honours).

My [job market paper](/assets/pdf/building_success_jmp.pdf) uses a 17-year panel of U.S. colleges to study how expansions in on-campus housing affect student retention and graduation. You can find my full [CV here](/cv/) and my other working papers on the [Research page](/publications/).

I am on the academic and policy-research job market. Feel free to reach out at [ciaran.murphy10@ucdconnect.ie](mailto:ciaran.murphy10@ucdconnect.ie).
